PREAMABLE

The short version of the code summarizes aspirations at a high level of abstraction. The clauses that
are included in the full version give examples and details of how these aspirations change the way we
act as software engineering professionals. Without the aspirations, the details can become legalistic
and tedious; without the details, the aspirations can become high-sounding but empty; together, the
aspirations and the details from cohesive code.
